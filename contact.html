Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common things customers ask us.

Ordering is easy! You can place an order online through our Shop page, or visit us in-store at our Barrie location. To order online, simply browse our selection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll be able to choose between convenient in-store pickup or local delivery.

We currently offer delivery within Barrie and the surrounding Simcoe County area, including:

  • Barrie city limits
  • Innisfil & Alcona
  • Midhurst & Minesing
  • Springwater & Oro-Medonte

Delivery is available Tuesday through Saturday. Minimum order of $25 applies. Contact us if you're outside our regular delivery zone — we may be able to accommodate special arrangements!

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your shopping experience as convenient as possible:

  • Visa, Mastercard, and American Express
  • Interac Debit and Credit
  • Cash (in-store only)
  • Interac e-Transfer (for delivery orders)
  • Google Pay and Apple Pay

All online payments are processed securely through our checkout system.

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with a purchase, let us know within 48 hours and we'll make it right. Perishable items must be returned same day. Non-perishable items in original condition can be exchanged or refunded within 7 days of purchase. Please bring your receipt or order confirmation when requesting a return.
