Bringing Flavours of Home to Barrie

Eden African & Caribbean Groceries is your neighbourhood source for authentic ingredients from across Africa and the Caribbean. From the freshest tropical produce to hard-to-find spices, frozen specialties, and traditional bakery items — we stock what you remember from home.

We're proud to serve Barrie's growing Caribbean and African community. Whether you're cooking a Saturday jerk chicken, a hearty egusi soup, or just want fresh coconut water — we've got you covered.
