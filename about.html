Our Story

Bringing the authentic flavours of Africa and the Caribbean to Barrie, Ontario.

Bringing Flavours of Home to Barrie

Eden African & Caribbean Groceries was born from a simple observation: Barrie's growing Caribbean and African community deserved a grocery store that truly understands their culinary traditions. Too often, finding authentic ingredients meant driving to Toronto or settling for substitutes.

We opened our doors to change that. Every product on our shelves — from fresh Scotch bonnet peppers to premium red palm oil, from frozen mackerel to hard dough bread — is carefully selected to bring the tastes of home to your kitchen.

Whether you're Nigerian, Jamaican, Ghanaian, Trinidadian, or simply someone who loves vibrant, flavourful cooking — you're family here.

Our Journey

How Eden Groceries grew from an idea to Barrie's destination for authentic ingredients.

2023 — The Idea

Seeing the Need

After years of watching friends and family drive to Toronto for African and Caribbean ingredients, our founder realized Barrie needed its own source for authentic groceries.

2024 — Opening Day

Doors Open in Barrie

Eden African & Caribbean Groceries opened its doors in Barrie. The response was overwhelming — customers came from across Simcoe County, grateful for the selection and service.

2025 — Going Digital

Online Ordering Launches

We launched our online ordering system, making it easy for customers to browse our full selection, place orders, and choose pickup or delivery — all from their phone or computer.

2026 — Growing Together

Expanding Our Reach

Today, we stock over 1,000 products sourced from more than 50 countries. We continue to expand our selection based on customer requests, and we're proud to serve Barrie's growing community.
